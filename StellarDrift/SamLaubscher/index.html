INSTRUCTIONS:
Have you got the skills to maneuver the ship as you race through the asteroid field to reach the winning score of 10,000? As your score increases, so will your speed! Watch for the colours changing to indicate your progression!

CONTROLS:
To control the ship, use the left and right arrow keys, or for mobile and tablet users, touch anywhere on the the left and right sides of the screen. When you're ready, hit the Enter key or press the Start Game button to begin... good luck!
