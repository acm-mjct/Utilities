Match The Card






INSTRUCTIONS

It is a classic memory game. You are given
twenty cards faced down and time limit of 100
seconds. Click the cards to flip them. There
are 10 avatars (each twice) distributed among
the 20 cards. Match each of the 10 avatar pairs
by flipping the cards. If any pair don't match both
the cards will flip again. You lose if you fail to
match all the cards before the timer runs out.
